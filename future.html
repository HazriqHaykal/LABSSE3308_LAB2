My Vision for the Future .

As a PNB scholar, I aim to build my career with Permodalan Nasional Berhad. PNB is one of Malaysia’s leading fund management companies, dedicated to empowering Bumiputera equity participation and supporting national growth. I’m inspired by their mission and look forward to contributing my skills and knowledge to support their long-term vision.

Once I’ve gained valuable experience at PNB, my long-term goal is to launch my own tech startup. I hope to apply the professional skills, industry knowledge, and strategic thinking I’ve developed to build innovative digital solutions that address real-world problems. My vision is to create technology that not only drives business growth but also supports Malaysia’s progress in becoming a competitive player in the global digital economy. I’m especially passionate about leveraging technology to improve lives, increase efficiency, and promote sustainable development across sector
