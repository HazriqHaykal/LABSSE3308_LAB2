My achievements

My journey through high school was anything but easy, especially with the challenges brought by the COVID-19 pandemic during my Form 4 and Form 5 years. Despite the uncertainties and disruptions, I remained determined and worked hard to achieve my goals. In my SPM, I proudly secured 9As and 1B+, and I was honored with the Best Subject Award for Biology, History, and Tasawwur Islam.

  • Best SPM 2021 Result

  • Best Biology Subject SPM 2021 Result

  • Best Sejarah Subject SPM 2021 Result

  • Best Tasawwur Islam Subject SPM 2021 Result

It was an honor to receive an award from the former Ketua Polis Negara for achieving excellent results in my SPM. Shaking hands with him was a truly memorable moment. His name is Tan Sri Dato' Seri Panglima Acryl Sani bin Haji Abdullah Sani, and I feel privileged to have had the opportunity to meet him in person.

My first and second semesters in my Software Engineering degree were tough, but I managed to make it to the Dean's List for both semesters. It was an incredible moment to be recognized at the Majlis Anugerah Prestij Fakulti. What made it even more special was celebrating this achievement with my friends and coursemates.

Receiving the Permodalan Nasional Berhad (PNB) Scholarship has been the biggest achievement of my academic journey. I am incredibly grateful to have successfully passed all four stages of the interview process. This scholarship has strengthened my determination to study harder and make the most of this opportunity.
